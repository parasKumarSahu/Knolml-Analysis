b"Air Liberia was a domestic airline based in Liberia. Formed in 1974 following a name change from Liberian National Airways, the carrier ceased trading in 1990.\nIt initially operated a prop fleet (Cessna 337's) joined by Britten-Norman Islander & Trislander aircraft in 1975 for scheduled services alongside Boeing 737 which was used for government VIP operations.\n\nAccidents and incidents\nOn 19 April 1975, Douglas C-47A EL-AAB was damaged beyond economic repair in a take-off accident at Roberts International Airport, Harbel. All 25 people on board survived.\n\nReferences\nExternal links\nAir Liberia logo, Museum of Flight"
