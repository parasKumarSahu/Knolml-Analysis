b'Aircraft Designs Inc is a US aircraft design and manufacturing firm founded in Monterey, California by Martin Hollmann in 1976. Apart from working on its own designs, the firm provides design and engineering analysis to other aerospace ventures.Hollmann and his company carried out much subcontract work, particularity in his areas of expertise, including aeroelasticity and structural analysis.
