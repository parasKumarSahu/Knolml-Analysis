b'Isamu Ueda (\xe4\xb8\x8a\xe7\x94\xb0 \xe5\x8b\x87, Ueda Isamu, born 1958) is a Japanese politician of the New Komeito Party, a member of the House of Representatives in the Diet (national legislature). A native of Yokohama, Kanagawa and graduate of the University of Tokyo, he worked at the Ministry of Agriculture, Forestry and Fisheries from 1981 to 1993.
