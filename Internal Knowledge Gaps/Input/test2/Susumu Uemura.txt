b'Susumu Uemura (\xe6\xa4\x8d\xe6\x9d\x91 \xe6\x99\x8b, Uemura Susumu, born April 9, 1964) is a former Japanese football player.\n\nPlaying career\nUemura was born in Shiga Prefecture on April 9, 1964. After graduating from Doshisha University, he played for Gamba Osaka. In J.League first season in 1993, he played opening match against Urawa Reds on May 16. He played as defender in 5 consecutive games from the opening game.
